Emotion

Animated chat for animated people

Welcome!

Emotion is a place for people to come together and talk about almost anything. Using the power of two second webcam recordings, it can bring about a much deeper sense of connection than most other Internet communication mediums. Say hi, stay a while!

Guidelines

  • Show your face! You'll get along much better here if you do.
  • Be kind. This is a great space, full of welcoming, interesting people, many of whom have been here a long time. We're interested in fostering a community that people want to come back to often. We hope you are, too!
  • Participate! This experience is better for everyone when you don't just sit back and watch from the sidelines. Get in there and make some GIFs!
